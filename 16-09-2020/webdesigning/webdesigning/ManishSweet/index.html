Our Story

In the year 1965 Vitthalbhai Haribhai Hadvani started manufacturing traditional Namkeen in village Bhadra, district Rajkot. With the clear vision and boundless enthusiasm of his son Bipinbhai Vitthalbhai Hadvani, The foundation stone of Manish Namkeen was laid in 1994 and the legacy is continued till date.
